## 🔴 CRITICAL HIRES (POST WEEK 1)

### Job 1: Tech Lead / Engineering Manager

**Title:** Tech Lead / Engineering Manager

**Reporting:** CTO or VP Engineering

**Contract:** 3-6 months (full-time)

**Salary:** R$ 15.000 - 25.000/month (based on experience)

**Location:** São Paulo or Remote (Brazil timezone preferred)

---

**About the Role:**

We're building 4 major features to compete with pganalyze (market leader in PostgreSQL monitoring). You'll lead a 9-person team across backend, frontend, database, and DevOps, shipping Query Performance Analysis, Log Intelligence, Index Advisor, and VACUUM Advisor in 14 weeks.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Overall vision and strategy for feature rollout
- Team leadership (1:1s, feedback, growth)
- Sprint planning and execution
- Blocker removal and risk management
- Final code review approvals
- Release planning and communication
- Metrics and progress tracking
- Hiring and onboarding oversight

**Required Skills:**
- 8+ years software engineering experience
- Experience leading small teams (5-10 people)
- Strong Go and/or Java/Python background
- PostgreSQL intermediate+ knowledge
- React/Frontend understanding (not expert)
- System design and architecture skills
- Clear communicator
- Remote/async work experience

**Nice to Have:**
- PostgreSQL monitoring/tools experience
- Startup/fast-paced environment
- Open source contribution
- Brazilian market knowledge

**What You'll Deliver:**
- Week 1-4: Onboard team, remove blockers
- Week 5-8: Ship v3.1.0 (Query Performance)
- Week 9-11: Ship v3.2.0 (Log Analysis)
- Week 12-13: Ship v3.3.0 (Index Advisor)
- Week 14: Ship v3.4.0 (VACUUM Advisor)

**Interview Process:**
1. Phone screen (30 min)
2. Technical interview (60 min) - architecture/design
3. Leadership interview (60 min) - team/communication
4. Reference check
5. Offer

**Timeline:**
- Applications review: April 1-3
- Phone screens: April 4-6
- Final interviews: April 7-10
- Start: April 20 (Week 4)

### Job 2: Senior Database Administrator (DBA)

**Title:** Senior Database Administrator / Database Architect

**Reporting:** Tech Lead

**Contract:** 3-month engagement (full-time, ~70% allocation)

**Salary:** R$ 12.000 - 18.000/month

**Location:** São Paulo or Remote

---

**About the Role:**

We're implementing 4 major monitoring features that require robust database design, performance optimization, and reliability. You'll design schemas for:
- Query performance tracking (1M+ events/day)
- Log analysis (10M+ logs/day)
- Index recommendations
- VACUUM advisor

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Database schema design for new features
- Performance optimization and tuning
- Migration planning and execution
- Data integrity and consistency
- Backup/recovery procedures
- Production database management
- Performance baselines and monitoring
- Capacity planning

**Required Skills:**
- 10+ years PostgreSQL experience
- Advanced query optimization
- Schema design and normalization
- Replication and HA experience
- Linux/Unix sysadmin basics
- Performance analysis tools
- Scripting (Bash/Python/SQL)
- Data consistency understanding

**What You'll Do:**
- Week 1-4: Onboard, database planning
- Week 5-8: Design 4 tables for Query Performance
  - Hypertable optimization
  - Compression setup
  - Retention policies
- Week 9-11: Design 4 tables for Log Analysis
  - High-volume table design
  - Partitioning strategy
  - Index optimization
- Week 12-13: Design 3 tables for Index Advisor
  - Recommendations tracking
  - Historical analysis
  - Performance impact measurement

**Interview Process:**
1. Phone screen (30 min)
2. Technical interview (90 min) - schema design challenge
3. Scenario interview (60 min) - real-world problems
4. Reference check
5. Offer

**Timeline:**
- Applications: April 1-3
- Phone screens: April 4-5
- Final interviews: April 6-8
- Start: April 20 (Week 4)
