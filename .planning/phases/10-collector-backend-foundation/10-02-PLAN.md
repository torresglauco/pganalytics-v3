PostgreSQL system views for logical replication:
- pg_stat_subscription (PG 10+): subscription_name, status, received_lsn, latest_end_lsn
- pg_publication: pubname, pubowner, puballtables, pubinsert/pubupdate/pubdelete
- pg_stat_wal_receiver (PG 9.6+): status, sender_host, sender_port, received_lsn

For cascading topology:
- Primary: has entries in pg_stat_replication (sends WAL)
- Standby: has entry in pg_stat_wal_receiver (receives WAL)
- Cascading standby: receives from another standby, not primary
